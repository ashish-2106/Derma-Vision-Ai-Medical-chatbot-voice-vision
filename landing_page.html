Frequently Asked Questions

Is Derma Vision a replacement for seeing a doctor?

+

No, Derma Vision is designed for educational purposes and preliminary assessment only. Always consult with a qualified healthcare professional for proper diagnosis and treatment of medical conditions.

How accurate is the AI analysis?

+

While our AI model is trained on a large dataset of skin conditions, it should be used as a supportive tool rather than definitive diagnosis. The accuracy varies depending on image quality and the specific condition.

Is my data kept private and secure?

+

Yes, we take data privacy seriously. All images and voice recordings are encrypted and stored securely. Your data is never shared with third parties without your explicit consent.

What skin conditions can Derma Vision detect?

+

Derma Vision can provide insights on common skin conditions including acne, eczema, psoriasis, rosacea, fungal infections, and various rashes. However, it's important to remember that it's not designed to diagnose serious conditions like skin cancer.
